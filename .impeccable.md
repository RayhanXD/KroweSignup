## Design Context

### Users
Early-stage founders at various experience levels — technical builders, non-technical entrepreneurs, and everyone in between. They've just completed the Krowe signup flow and are now in the interview analysis workspace. Their mindset: goal-oriented and analytical, wanting to validate their idea through real user data. They need the interface to feel smart without being overwhelming.

### Brand Personality
Intelligent, clear, confident. The product helps founders make better decisions — the UI should reflect that: authoritative but not intimidating, clean but not sterile.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light mode. Clean, professional, and elevated — not generic. White backgrounds with zinc neutrals. Orange (#f97316) is the required brand color, used purposefully for primary CTAs and active states only — not decorative. Typography: Geist Sans (already loaded). Anti-reference: avoid dark mode, avoid the generic "AI startup" purple-cyan palette.

### Design Principles
1. **Orange earns its place** — only use orange for primary actions (CTAs) and active/selected states. Never decorative.
2. **Signal over noise** — surfaces should be clean with generous whitespace. No clutter. Dense data (decision outputs) should feel structured, not overwhelming.
3. **Confidence by design** — status indicators, progress markers, and data displays should feel authoritative and readable at a glance.
4. **Standard with craft** — "standard UI" means familiar patterns (lists, tabs, forms) executed with careful spacing, typography hierarchy, and consistent detail.
5. **Hierarchy through type** — use Geist Sans weight variation (400/500/600/700) to create clear visual hierarchy. No monospace for UI chrome.
